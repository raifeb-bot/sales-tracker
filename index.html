Checking sync…

Enter your key once on this phone — employees use the crew key, the owner uses the owner key (ask the owner if you don't have it). After that, every sale automatically uploads and downloads about once a minute whenever you're online. Crew members can add sales and change only their own entries; the owner can edit everything.

How your data is stored

Every sale saves on this device instantly, even with no signal, and syncs to the team when you're back online. The backup buttons below are your safety net: export everything as a file, or merge in a teammate's file by hand. Merging is safe to repeat — duplicates are matched up, and the newest edit of each sale wins.

Export
